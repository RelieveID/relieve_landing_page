Introducing... Relieve

a Disaster Ready Family App

Be Aware of your Surroundings 

We help you find out about your surroundings in real-time. Is there any disaster around.

Access Actual Informations

Actual information update all about disaster. Where was it, when, and more information if you want to find out 

Family Monitor

Providing the current state of your family,
You can add person into your family then monitor their condition, simply by a quick glimpse. 

Optimized for Families

We bring save and peace around your family. Designed simple and comfy for any member of your family.

Help you aware

Relieve helps you aware, disaster is an unknown event but a most-ready event.

Get notify if disaster happens

We will prompt you, when there is a disaster near you and ask you whether you are save or not. We also automatically inform it to your families, so they wouldn’t be in panic

Make sure they save

Providing possible affected family member in certain location when disaster happens. And if you worried about your familiy, just ping them or have a call.

All you need is here

When you need help, we provide you with some emergency phone numbers that you can contact. Don’t worry we can give you multiple number ordered by your distance to the location
